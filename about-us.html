About Us

BG-Energy Tech is an organisation born out of the need to proffer pragmatic, feasible and realistic solutions to the problems threatening our environment and the problems associated with power generation and distribution in Nigeria. The core of our business is the production of biogas for cooking, power Generation and biogas fertilizer(a byproduct of biogas production). We also specialize in the training of individuals and corporate bodies in the production of bio-gas and offer quality consultancy services. We want to build a legacy of adopting and adapting existing innovations that can bring about sustainable growth and development to our country Nigeria.
